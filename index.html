Before the chapters

How you became my person

Somewhere between confiding in you, exploring a zoo, staring at a hippo, and laughing through the kind of odd little conversation only we could have, I found the person I did not even know I was hoping for. You feel like a blessing in the middle of everything, the one good thing I know how to hold onto right now.

Your smile melts my heart, and because you smile so often, my poor heart barely gets a break. The day you went to Kolkata, I missed you in a way I had never missed anyone before. I just wanted time to hurry up until you were back.

Then you smiled, and life quietly became warmer.

Chapter I

A walk in the park

Our first date felt like the world was trying to leave us a sign. Two cats appeared out of nowhere and decided to follow us, and yes, credit where it is due, you spotted one of them first. It was sweet, absurd, and exactly the kind of memory I want to keep forever.

Then came the butter dosa absolutely overloaded with butter. I am still sorry for that. I should have asked what you wanted instead of confidently leading us into a plate that was doing a little too much. Still, somehow, even that became ours.

Chapter II

Lab coat, lunch, and a hippo

I picked you up from the hospital in your lab coat, and I do not think I will ever forget how happy that made me. We went for lunch together, then to the zoo, because apparently I needed to prove that we really did have a hippo here.

It became the best date ever in the most effortless way. Just you, me, a zoo, one very important hippo, and that feeling that I could have stayed beside you for the whole day and still wanted more time.

Best date ever. No further evidence required.

Chapter III

Rain walks, movies, and everything next

After that, there were so many little pieces of us: walks after the rain, walks in the rain, the movie date, our day together, and of course me making you try the best golgappas in town. Right. Very obviously, unquestionably, the best.

And this is still just the beginning. There are more days coming, more months, more years, more ordinary moments that I know you will somehow make unforgettable. Be exactly the person you are. Keep that smile on your face. I love you.

Tiny proof that I look better simply by standing near you. Very unfair, very effective.
